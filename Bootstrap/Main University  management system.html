In view of the critical times being faced worldwide due the pandemic COVID-19, the University is also working dedicatedly towards ensuring the health and safety of its students and providing all necessary guidance and assistance to them.

In a step towards this initiative, the Summer break for the students has been preponed for their convenience, in order to reduce their travel costs and botheration. The exact joining date for the students shall be shared in due course of time.

In view of the above, the information regarding various academic activities is given below.

1. The Term-II of session 2019-20 (Spring Term 19202), as informed earlier, shall close as per the programme wise academic calendar and the students shall continue their study through online academic connect for the remaining time period of the term.

2. The End Term Examination (theory and practical) (for Spring Term 19202) shall be scheduled just before the commencement of new session i.e. Autumn Term 20211.

3. Tentatively, the End Term Examination-19202 shall be scheduled from the 3rd week of June 2020 onwards, however, the exact date sheet and schedule for ETE-19202 shall be conveyed to students at least 15 days before the start of exams, in accordance to the guidelines issued by statutory/ regulatory bodies.

4. The students are advised to make their travel arrangements only after the exact schedule is shared with them by the University.

5. The syllabus coverage and unit wise weightages for the End Term Examination (theory and practical) shall be informed to the students shortly via a separate communication.

6. Viva Examinations for Capstone/Dissertations/Trainings/Internships shall also be scheduled during the time period of End Term Examination- 19202.

7. The criteria for final attendance calculation for Spring Term 19202 shall be as per the directives issued by government or regulatory bodies and will be informed to the students in due course of time.

8. The next term i.e. Term-I of session 2020-21 (Autumn Term 20211) shall commence immediately after the completion of End Term Examination (theory) of Term-II of session 2019-20 (Spring Term 19202).

9. Hence, the students are advised to carry all the study material, belongings and other necessities required by them for the next session when they report to the University for ETE-19202, as they shall not have any time to go back to their hometown/native places after the completion of exams. They shall be required to join the classes of next session i.e. term 20211 immediately after completion of examination process of term 19202.

Students should keep themselves regularly updated through UMS for exact schedule.

The students are further advised to utilise the time of summer break for efficient preparation of their examination. The University staff/faculty members shall be available via LPU-UMS and LPU-LIVE App for all kinds of assistance required by the students.
